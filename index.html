Lost in the woods

You went for a summer hiking trip and got lost in the woods.

You follow a path you find and you end up at a fork in the road.

You come across an abandoned cabin.

You enter the abandonded cabin.

You rest in the cabin.

However, the cabin is haunted! You are now cursed.

You investigate.

You find a map that shows the way out the forest!

You come across a cave.

You explore the cave.

You delve deeper into the cave.

After exploring for hours, you come across an exit and find civilization once again.

You rested in the cave.

Unfortunately, you got mauled by a bear while sleeping.
